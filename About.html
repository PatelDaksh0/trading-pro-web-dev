Our Story, Mission & Values

Empowering traders with innovative technology, transparent pricing, and exceptional service since 2010.

Who We Are

TradeMaster Pro was founded in 2010 by a team of Wall Street veterans who believed the financial markets should be accessible to everyone, not just institutional investors.

What started as a small trading platform with just 5 employees has grown into one of the most trusted names in online trading, serving over 500,000 clients worldwide.

Our mission is to democratize finance through technology, education, and fair pricing. We're committed to providing the tools and resources traders need to succeed in today's fast-moving markets.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our company's history

2010 - Founded

Launched our first trading platform with just 5 employees in New York City, focusing on stock trading for retail investors.

2013 - Mobile Platform

Released our first mobile trading app, allowing clients to trade from anywhere. Reached 50,000 active users.

2015 - Global Expansion

Expanded operations to Europe and Asia, adding forex and commodities trading to our platform.

2017 - Crypto Trading

Launched cryptocurrency trading with 10 major coins. Surpassed 200,000 active traders worldwide.

2023 - Today

Recognized as one of the top 3 trading platforms globally with over 500,000 clients and 300 employees across 15 countries.
